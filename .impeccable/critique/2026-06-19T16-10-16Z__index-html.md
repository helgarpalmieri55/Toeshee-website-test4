## Anti-Patterns Verdict
**LLM:** Not slop. Restrained boutique-institutional voice, single disciplined orange accent, sourced proof, confidential-client convention. Residue: 3 of 5 insight titles use the same "Four Patterns/Questions That Separate…" listicle mold (off-voice).
**Detector:** exit 0, **clean** — the prior em-dash-overuse is resolved.
**Overlays:** not available (no browser automation) — live contrast over video and mobile framing remain source-only judgments.

## What's Working
1. Voice discipline carried into the highest-stakes interaction (form/error copy sounds like a senior operator).
2. Honest, structured form architecture: validation, aria-invalid, role=alert/status, focus-to-first-invalid, fetch-or-simulate fork, human email fallback on error.
3. Proof integrity + accent restraint: differentiated about-stats, sourced numbers, single-accent system makes the proof drench land.

## Priority Issues (remaining)
**[P0] Form shows fake success with no wired endpoint.** `data-endpoint` is unset, so every real submit takes the 1s simulate branch and says "Message sent" though nothing sends. *Fix:* wire an endpoint, or make the no-endpoint branch truthful (direct to email) instead of a success.
**[P1] Success dead-ends the form.** Button becomes "Sent" + disabled with no reset; a second inquiry needs a reload. *Fix:* re-enable on success or on next field input.
**[P1] Nav dropdown-parent parity.** Desktop Solutions/Industries are toggle-only buttons; mobile menu links them directly. *Fix:* let desktop parents navigate to the index page while still opening the panel on hover/focus.
**[P2] Reused team photo** across About + Careers (+ near-identical contact shot) undercuts a specificity-first pitch.
**[P2] Thin proof sources** — 96% ("Post-deployment average") and 60% ("Confidential iGaming client") are non-attributable; add methodology/date.
**[P2] Insight titles read as AI-listicle.** Rewrite to concrete operator headlines.
**[P3] Help gap at conversion** — add a one-line privacy note / response SLA under submit.
